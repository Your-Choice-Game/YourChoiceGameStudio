Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to all games developed by Your Choice Game Studio ("we," "us," or "our"), including but not limited to Super Words Puzzle, Super Bubble Shooter, Super Aqua Sort, Super Hunter Saga, Super Mahjong, Super Block, and any future games (collectively, the "Games") available on the Google Play Store. By playing our Games, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Account Details: Username, email address, or password when you create an account in any of our Games.
  • In-Game Content: Chat messages, player profiles, or feedback submitted within the Games.
  • Purchases: Payment details for in-game purchases, processed by third-party providers like Google Play (we do not store full payment information).

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Gameplay Data: Scores, progress, achievements, and player interactions across our Games.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, IP address, or unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Session duration, features used, and interactions within the Games.
  • Cookies/Trackers: Cookies or similar technologies for analytics and ads (e.g., via Google AdMob or in-game ad networks).

c. Third-Party Data

  • Social Logins: Name or email from platforms like Google or Facebook if you log in via these services.
  • Analytics/Ads: Aggregated data from analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) or ad networks for usage insights and targeted ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate and enhance our Games, including personalizing gameplay experiences.
  • Process in-game purchases and deliver rewards.
  • Communicate with you, such as for support or Game updates.
  • Analyze usage to improve Game performance and features.
  • Deliver relevant ads via Google AdMob or other networks (if applicable).
  • Prevent fraud and ensure Game security.
  • Comply with legal obligations, including Google Play Store policies.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Vendors for hosting, analytics, or payment processing (e.g., Google Play).
  • Ad Partners: Non-personal data for targeted advertising (e.g., via Google AdMob).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: If our studio or Games are sold or merged.
  • With Consent: For features like multiplayer or social connections across our Games.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Games are not intended for children under 13 (or 16 where required by law, such as under GDPR or COPPA). We do not knowingly collect data from children under these ages. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly. Contact us at support@yourchoicegamestudio.com with concerns.

5. Your Choices

You may:

  • Adjust settings in our Games (e.g., disable chat or opt out of personalized ads).
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing support@yourchoicegamestudio.com.
  • Opt out of targeted ads via Google Play Store settings or in-game options (where available).

6. Data Security

We use encryption and other measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as needed to support Game functionality (e.g., saving progress) or comply with legal requirements, such as Google Play Store policies.

8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country (e.g., in the U.S.) to support our Games. We use safeguards like Standard Contractual Clauses to protect it during transfers.

9. Third-Party Links

Our Games may include links to external services (e.g., Google Play, social platforms). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new Games or changes in practices. Significant updates will be communicated via the Games, our website, or email.
